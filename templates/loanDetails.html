Loans for Farmers

Loan for Financing Farmers for Purchase of Land (Agricultural Loan by Bank of Maharashtra)

Eligibility Criteria:
- Small & Marginal Farmers (Who owns maximum of 5 acre of non irrigated land or 2.5 acres of irrigated land including land to be purchase)
- Share Croppers/tenant farmers cultivating up to 2.5 acres of irrigated land or 5 acres of unirrigated land
- Entrepreneurs with agricultural background are also eligible (Provided state laws permits purchase of agri lands by non agriculturists)

Kisan Samriddhi Rin (Crop Loan by State Bank of India)

Eligibility Criteria:
- Farmers engaged in progressive or Scientific farming.
- Minimum land holding: At least having 4 acres of land holding Or farmer is engaged in scientific methods of farming.
- Credit score: 650 and above · Age Criteria: Min. 18 years, Max. 70 years. (Above 60 yrs., Co-borrower is must).

Finance To Poultry Sector (Livestock Loan by Union Bank of India)

Eligibility Criteria:
- Individual/ Joint individuals/Proprietor concern/Partnership firm /LLP /Private ltd Company/Public Limited Company experienced in the line of poultry farming and competent to run the business.
- The unit should be technically feasible and economically viable.
- Other constitutions such as group of individuals, Associations, Co-operatives, Federations, FPOs can also be financed for raising poultry.
