Discover Schemes for Farmers

Empowering farmers through government initiatives for a better tomorrow.

Explore Schemes

Here’s a list of important schemes designed to provide financial aid, insurance, and resources for Indian farmers. Explore how these can benefit your farming practices.

Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN)

Provides ₹6,000 per year to small and marginal farmers, credited in three installments.

  • Eligibility: Small and marginal farmers.
Learn More

Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)

A crop insurance scheme to protect farmers against crop loss due to natural calamities.

  • Eligibility: All farmers who have taken loans for notified crops.
Learn More

Soil Health Card Scheme

Provides farmers with soil health cards to improve soil fertility.

  • Eligibility: All farmers across India.
Learn More

Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana (PKVY)

Promotes organic farming through the development of clusters and Farmer Producer Organizations (FPOs).

  • Eligibility: Farmers interested in organic farming.
Learn More

Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchai Yojana (PMKSY)

A scheme aimed at improving irrigation facilities for farmers to enhance productivity & ensure water conservation.

  • Eligibility: Any farmer can apply for benefits under this scheme.
Learn More
